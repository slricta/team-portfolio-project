Bachelor Of Science In Secondary Education

All Academic Programs


Strand Alignment:

  • General Academic Strand (GAS)
  • Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)
  • Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)
    • The Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSE) is an undergraduate teacher education program designed to equip learners with adequate and relevant competencies to teach in their chosen area of specialization/major in the secondary level.

    • The BSE degree program aims to develop highly motivated and competent teachers specializing in the content and pedagogy for secondary education.

    • Specific Professions/Careers/Occupations for graduates:

    • After successful completion of all academic requirements for the degree/program, graduates of BSE should be able to practice the teaching profession in the Secondary Level.
