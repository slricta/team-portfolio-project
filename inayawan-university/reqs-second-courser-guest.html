Admission for Second Courser

All Requirements and Procedures


  1. Wait for the announcement regarding the schedule of the application for admission at our Facebook page or this website
  2. Fill up the application form in the admission portal
  3. Submission and Evaluation of the following documents for Application for Entrance Examination
    • Accomplished application form
    • Transcript of Records
    • 1 x 1 picture
  4. Entrance Examination
  5. Issuance of Notice of Admission (NOA) for qualified applicants
  6. Interview at the Department of your chosen course
  7. For accepted applicants, secure a medical referral slip from the Health Service Unit and proceed to medical examination
  8. Claim the medical clearance at the Health Service Unit
  9. Submit the following requirements to the Office of the Campus Registrar to process your enrollment
    • Transcript of Records
    • Notice of Admission(NOA)
    • Medical Clearance
    • Short brown envelope (Indicate Last name, First name, Program Applied at the back)
